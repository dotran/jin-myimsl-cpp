pljoin: Draw a line between two points

pljoin (x1, y1, x2, y2);

Joins the point (x1, y1) to (x2, y2).

x1 (PLFLT, input)

x coordinate of first point.

y1 (PLFLT, input)

y coordinate of first point.

x2 (PLFLT, input)

x coordinate of second point.

y2 (PLFLT, input)

y coordinate of second point.
